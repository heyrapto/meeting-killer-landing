How It Works
Section label: How it works

Headline: Simple for the team. Useful for managers.

Step 1: Post your update Team members submit a daily update in text, image, audio, or video before the workspace deadline.

Step 2: AI structures the signal Meeting Killer processes the update and turns it into a clean summary with completed work, ongoing work, and blockers.

Step 3: Everyone stays aligned Updates appear in the team feed and can be shared to Slack so progress stays visible without another meeting.
